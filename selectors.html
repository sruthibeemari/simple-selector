Making of Tea

1.Heat Water: Heat fresh water in a pot or kettle.

2.Add Tea Leaves: Add one teaspoon of loose-leaf tea or one tea bag for every 8 oz of water.

3. Steep: Pour hot water over the tea leaves and let it steep for 2-5 minutes.

4.Add Milk: Add milk (dairy or non-dairy) to the tea, holding back the foam with a spoon.

5.Add Sugar (Optional): Add sugar or honey to taste, if desired.

Making of Dosa

1.Prepare Batter: Mix dosa batter mix with water to form a smooth paste.

2.Heat Pan: Heat a non-stick pan or dosa tava over medium heat.

3.Pour Batter: Pour a ladle of batter onto the pan and spread evenly.

4.Cook Dosa: Cook for 1-2 minutes, until the edges start to curl and the surface is dry.

5.Flip Dosa: Flip the dosa and cook for another minute.

6.Serve Hot: Serve hot with sambar, chutney, and other desired fillings.

Making of Maggie

1.Boil Water: Boil 2 cups of water in a pot.

2.Add Noodles: Add 1 packet of Maggi noodles to the boiling water.

3.Cook Noodles: Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until the noodles are soft.

4.Add Seasoning: Add the seasoning packet that comes with Maggi noodles.

5.Stir Well: Stir well to combine the seasoning with the noodles.

6. Serve Hot: Serve hot and enjoy!
